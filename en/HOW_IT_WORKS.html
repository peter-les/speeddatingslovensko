What is speed dating?

  • Speed dating are dating evenings where you meet in person all participants of the opposite gender who attend the event.
  • Currently, we usually have 4 to 8 people of the opposite gender at our events.
  • After a short personal conversation in pairs, you must decide if you want to meet this person again in the future.
  • If yes, write it on paper, and in case of a mutual match, the moderator will send both of you the contacts after the event so you can continue meeting independently.

How does it work with us?

  • At the beginning, the moderator/organizer will greet you and ask for your first name.
    (In case of multiple matches, also your surname)
  • You will receive a paper with your first name, which you carry with you throughout the evening.
    (Bring your own paper for notes you want to take home)
  • Pens are prepared at eight tables.
  • Women sit at the tables and men sit opposite them.
  • After 6 minutes, a bell rings and men move one seat to the next table.
  • You have 6 minutes to decide whether you want to meet this person again in the future.
  • Write the names of the people you want to meet again on the paper.
  • The moderator will collect all papers at the end of the evening.
  • After the event, if there is a mutual match, the moderator will send the contacts to both parties.
